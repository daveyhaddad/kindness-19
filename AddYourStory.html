Add Your Story

Every single person can have a story of how someone has been kind to them
during this time and a story of how they have been kind to someone else.

To add your own story (or stories) of how someone has spread Kindness-19 with you, how you have spread it to someone else, or of any kindness that you have witnessed:

  1. Follow this link
  2. Click on the “Add Marker” icon in the top left of the page
  3. Click on a place on the map where you have given or received kindness (it doesn’t have to be exact if you don’t want to have your location seen)
  4. Add a title and a description of what happened and press “Save” when you are finished
  5. Your addition to the map should show up on this website in about 5 minutes
  6. Watch as kindness spreads and feel free to share this map with others!
